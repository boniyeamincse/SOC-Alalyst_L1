## [1.0.0] - 2024-06-22 - COMPLETE

### Released - Full 28-Module SOC L1 Course

**Complete Training Program:**
- ✅ 28 comprehensive modules
- ✅ 23 theoretical modules (fundamentals + skills)
- ✅ 4 practical labs (beginner → advanced)
- ✅ 1 capstone project (full shift simulation)
- ✅ 1 final assessment (30 questions)
- ✅ ~23,500 lines of content
- ✅ Bangla-English mixed language

**Module Breakdown:**

Modules 1-7: SOC Fundamentals
- Introduction to SOC, team structure, tools, events/logs/alerts, alert properties, prioritization, triage

Modules 8-16: Investigation Skills
- Verdicts, investigation methodology, identity/asset inventory, TI lookups, network diagrams, documentation, enrichment, SIEM

Modules 17-23: Professional Skills
- Alert scenarios, reporting, escalation, communication, metrics, improvement, resilience

Modules 24-27: Practical Application
- Beginner lab, intermediate lab, advanced lab, capstone simulation

Module 28: Final Assessment
- 30-question comprehensive exam
