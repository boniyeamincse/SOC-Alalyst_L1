TRUST ZONES:

Zone 1 - EXTERNAL (🔴 RED - UNTRUSTED)
  • Internet, attackers
  • All traffic blocked unless explicitly allowed
  • No direct access to internal systems

Zone 2 - DMZ (🟡 YELLOW - SEMI-TRUSTED)
  • Internet-facing servers
  • Limited access to internal network
  • Monitored heavily
  • Can be compromised, won't expose internal network

Zone 3 - INTERNAL (🟢 GREEN - TRUSTED)
  • Corporate network
  • Internal only access
  • Higher trust level
  • All devices monitored
  • Segmented by function (office, admin, servers, DB)

MONITORING:
  ✓ Firewall logs (all traffic)
  ✓ IDS/IPS (intrusion detection)
  ✓ EDR (endpoint detection & response)
  ✓ SIEM (security information)
  ✓ Network TAP (traffic analysis)
